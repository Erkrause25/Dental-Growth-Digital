Preventive Care

Keep Little Smiles Healthy

Prevention is the foundation of lifelong oral health. Our preventive services help protect your child's teeth from decay and establish healthy habits early.

  • Comprehensive Exams Thorough checkups to monitor growth, development, and overall oral health
  • Professional Cleanings Gentle cleanings to remove plaque and tartar, with tips for home care
  • Fluoride Treatments Strengthen tooth enamel and help prevent cavities
  • Dental Sealants Protective coating for molars to prevent decay in hard-to-brush areas
  • Digital X-Rays Low-radiation imaging to detect problems early

Restorative Dentistry

Gentle Repairs for Growing Smiles

When cavities or damage occur, we provide gentle, effective treatments to restore your child's teeth and keep them smiling confidently.

  • Tooth-Colored Fillings Natural-looking composite fillings that blend seamlessly with teeth
  • Stainless Steel Crowns Durable protection for baby teeth with extensive decay
  • Pulp Therapy Treatment for deep cavities that reach the tooth's nerve
  • Space Maintainers Preserve space for permanent teeth after early tooth loss
  • Extractions Gentle removal of severely damaged or infected teeth when necessary

Sedation Dentistry

Anxiety-Free Care for Every Child

Some children need a little extra help feeling comfortable at the dentist. We offer safe sedation options to ensure a positive, stress-free experience.

  • Nitrous Oxide (Laughing Gas) Mild sedation that helps children relax while remaining awake and responsive
  • Oral Conscious Sedation Deeper relaxation for anxious children or longer procedures
  • Hospital Dentistry General anesthesia available for extensive treatment or special needs patients

All sedation is performed by trained professionals with continuous monitoring for your child's safety.
