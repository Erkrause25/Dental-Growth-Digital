Our Story

A Practice Built on Caring for Kids

Happy Smiles Pediatric Dentistry was founded in 2009 with a simple mission: to provide exceptional dental care in an environment where children feel safe, comfortable, and even excited about their dental visits.

Dr. Sarah Chen started this practice after years of seeing children develop dental anxiety at traditional offices. She envisioned a space designed from the ground up with kids in mindâ€”from the colorful decor and kid-sized equipment to the gentle, patient approach every team member brings.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of families in Cherry Hill and the surrounding communities. Our practice has grown, but our commitment remains the same: giving every child the foundation for a lifetime of healthy smiles.

Our Values

What We Stand For

These core principles guide everything we do at Happy Smiles.

Compassionate Care

Every child is unique, and we take time to understand their needs, fears, and personality. We never rush, and we always prioritize comfort.

Fun & Positive

Dental visits should be something to look forward to! We create joyful experiences that help children build positive associations with oral health.

Excellence & Safety

We use the latest techniques and maintain the highest standards of safety and sterilization. Your child's health is our top priority.
