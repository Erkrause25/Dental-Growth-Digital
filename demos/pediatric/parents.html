First Visit Tips

Preparing Your Little One

Here's how to set your child up for a positive experience!

1

Stay Positive!

Talk about "counting teeth" not "shots." Your excitement is contagious!

2

Read Books

Kids books about dentists help make it familiar and fun.

3

Play Pretend

Practice opening wide with stuffed animals at home!

4

Time It Right

Book when your child is rested and happy.

FAQs

Questions Parents Ask

When should my child first visit?

By age 1 or when the first tooth appears - whichever comes first!

How often should we come?

Every 6 months for most kiddos. Some may need more frequent visits.

Are X-rays safe?

Yes! Digital X-rays use very low radiation and help us spot problems early.

My child is nervous. Help!

Totally normal! Our team specializes in anxious kids. We go at their pace.

Can I stay during the visit?

Absolutely! Parents are always welcome in the room with us.

What insurance do you take?

Most major plans including Delta, Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, and more.
