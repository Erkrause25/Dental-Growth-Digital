Age-Specific Care

Tailored for Every Age

Under 4: Lap Exams

For our tiniest patients, we do "lap exams" where your child sits on your lap facing you. Then they lay back with their head in Dr. Sarah's lap.

This position feels safe and lets us see everything while you hold their hands. It's quick, gentle, and works great for nervous little ones!

4 and Up: Big Kid Chair

Once kids are ready, they graduate to our special dental chair that goes up and down like a rocket ship!

They get to wear cool sunglasses, watch shows on the ceiling TV, and show off how wide they can open. Independence = confidence!

Preparation Tips

How to Prepare at Home

1

Stay Positive

Use happy words like "counting teeth" and "smile checkup"!

2

Read Books

Dentist-themed books help make it feel familiar.

3

Play Pretend

Practice opening wide with stuffed animals!

4

Avoid Negatives

Skip words like "hurt," "shot," or "pain."
