Questions

Invisalign FAQ

Common questions about Invisalign clear aligners.

How long does Invisalign treatment take?

Treatment time varies based on your specific needs, but most cases are completed in 12-18 months. Some minor cases can be finished in as little as 6 months.

How many hours a day do I wear my aligners?

For best results, wear your aligners 20-22 hours per day, removing them only to eat, drink (anything other than water), brush, and floss.

Will Invisalign affect my speech?

Most people adjust to their aligners within a day or two. You may notice a slight lisp initially, but it typically goes away quickly as you get used to wearing them.

Is Invisalign right for me?

Invisalign can treat most common orthodontic issues including crowding, spacing, overbite, underbite, and crossbite. The best way to find out is to schedule a free consultation.

Does insurance cover Invisalign?

Most dental insurance plans that cover orthodontics will cover Invisalign the same way they cover braces. We'll help you understand your benefits during your consultation.
