Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about orthodontic treatment.

How long does orthodontic treatment take?

Treatment time varies based on your specific needs, typically ranging from 12-24 months. During your consultation, Dr. Martinez will provide a more precise estimate for your case.

Do braces hurt?

You may experience some discomfort for a few days after getting braces or after adjustments. This is normal and can be managed with over-the-counter pain relievers. Most patients adapt quickly.

How often will I need appointments?

Most patients visit every 6-8 weeks for adjustments. Invisalign patients may have fewer visits. We work with your schedule to find convenient appointment times.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans with 0% interest financing. We also accept most insurance plans and can help you maximize your benefits.
