Frequently Asked Questions

What if I just want a website without Google Ads?

No problem! The Essential package is perfect for practices that just need a professional website. You can always add Google Ads management later if you decide you want it.

How does the Google Ads pricing work?

You pay Google directly for your ad budget (typically $400-$1,200/month), and you pay us a monthly management fee to run and optimize your campaigns. We handle everything - you just approve the budget.

Can I cancel Google Ads anytime?

Yes! There are no long-term contracts for our Google Ads management. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

What's included in maintenance?

Content updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, regular backups, and priority technical support. Keeps your site running smoothly.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We can split the one-time website cost into 3-6 monthly payments for qualified practices. Ask us about payment options during your consultation.

What if I need something custom?

We're flexible! These packages are starting points. We can customize any package or create a completely custom solution for your specific needs. Let's talk about what you need.
